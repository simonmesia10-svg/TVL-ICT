About TVL ICT

The TVL ICT strand prepares students for careers in information technology, including software development, network administration, and digital media.

Computer System Servicing

Learn to assemble, maintain, and repair computer systems.

Networking

Understand computer networks and network administration.

Troubleshooting

Diagnose and resolve computer hardware and software issues.

Maintenance

Perform preventive maintenance on computer systems.

OHS

Learn occupational health and safety practices in ICT.

Computer Configuration

Configure hardware and software for optimal performance.
