## 🌟 Welcome to TVL ICT

**Welcome to the Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Information and Communications Technology (TVL-ICT) website!** 🎓

This comprehensive platform is designed for TVL ICT students, educators, and anyone interested in pursuing a career in Information Technology. Here you'll find everything you need to succeed in the TVL ICT strand of the K-12 curriculum.

### What You'll Find Here:

- **📚 Subject Information** - Detailed overview of TVL ICT subjects including Computer Systems Servicing, Networking, Troubleshooting, and more  
- **🎥 Educational Resources** - Curated video tutorials and learning materials from trusted sources  
- **💬 Community Discussions** - Interactive comment sections powered by GitHub Discussions for questions and collaboration  
- **🛠️ Practical Skills** - Resources focused on hands-on ICT competencies and vocational training  

### About TVL ICT
The TVL-ICT strand prepares students for careers in the growing field of Information Technology. Through a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical skills, students learn essential competencies in computer hardware, software, networking, and digital media. This program bridges the gap between education and employment in the ICT industry.

Whether you're a student preparing for your future career, an educator looking for teaching resources, or someone exploring ICT opportunities, this website serves as your gateway to quality TVL ICT education and community.
